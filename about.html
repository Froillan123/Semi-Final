Physical libraries are not always accessible. There are times when students require a specific book for a reference, and there are many more when they need to meet certain requirements instantaneously. However, if libraries have set hours, how can they obtain it? Our group then came up with the concept for an online library.

What distinguishes it from a traditional library? Apart from the fact that it is "online," you may access it far more simply than with real ones. You have access to thousands of books in many categories here. Although there are already categories and sections for books, you can reach them more easily by typing the title or author of the book in the search field. This will provide results for that specific book or books that are related to what you are looking for.

Our Online Library was designed with our fellow students who wish to strive well in academics. We hoped to make your difficulties and burdens a little less intense. Along with wanting to support our fellow students, we also regard books so highly that we don't want them to be lost in the midst of the rapid technological advancements that have made everything accessible online. We have therefore decided to save the books and take action to disseminate it! Thus, this Online booking site was established.

The books are divided into the following categories: history, psychology, natural science, biography, self-help books, textbooks, and memoir. But! New information is available! FICTIONS were also added. Don't worry, everything is free, so sign up and enjoy yourself while learning!
